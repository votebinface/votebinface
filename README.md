## About the Campaign

This is a grassroots website dedicated to gathering support for **Count Binface** (a.k.a. Jon Tibbles) in the 2026 UK General Election for the Clacton constituency.

Count Binface is running as the people's candidate against establishment politics, taking on Nigel Farage and Reform UK with wit, satire, and genuine political engagement.

- **Anti-establishment voice** - A genuine alternative to traditional party politics

- **Taking on the status quo** - Standing up to Farage and Reform's dominance
